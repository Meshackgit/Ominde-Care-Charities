Our Story

Ominde Charities, Non-Profit Organization

Ominde Care Charities stands as a beacon of hope, dedicated to uplifting the lives of the less fortunate. Through our multifaceted approach, we address various societal challenges head-on. Our commitment extends from aiding the homeless and empowering street kids to ensuring every child has access to education. Moreover, we actively engage in environmental conservation by planting trees, safeguarding our planet for future generations. However, our impact thrives through collective effort. Whether through volunteering, donations, or spreading awareness, every contribution matters. Together, we can create meaningful change and provide a brighter tomorrow for those in need. Join us in our mission to build a world where compassion reigns supreme.

Our Mission

Cultivating a culture of giving and solidarity by engaging volunteers, donors, and advocates to support our mission of creating a more equitable and compassionate society.
